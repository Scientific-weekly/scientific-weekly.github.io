Thought Provoking Questions

These questions are taken mainly from Reddit/r/askscience. Some example questions:

  • 1. If the Universe were shrunk to something akin to the size of Earth, what would the scale for stars, planets, etc. be?
  • 2. Why do artillery guns always shoot short at night?
  • 3. Why do we get allergies? Is there an evolutionary reason/benefit for why they exist?
  • 4. When we have to fight ourselves awake, what are we fighting exactly?
